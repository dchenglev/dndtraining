🎲 What is D&D?

Dungeons & Dragons is the world's greatest tabletop roleplaying game. One player is the Dungeon Master — they narrate the world. Everyone else plays a hero with unique abilities, choices, and a story to shape.

📚 How This Academy Works

  • Pick a campaign and enter the story
  • Follow the tutorial to learn the rules
  • Build your custom character
  • Practice in scenario simulations
  • Ask your AI Coach anything

🧙 Core Roles

  • Dungeon Master — runs the world and NPCs
  • Fighter — frontline warrior and protector
  • Wizard — arcane spellcaster
  • Rogue — cunning trickster and scout
  • Cleric — divine healer and support

🎯 Start Here

  • Never played? Open The Shattered Prophecy
  • Build a character before your first session
  • Use the simulator to practice decisions
  • Check Faction Codex to learn lore & factions
